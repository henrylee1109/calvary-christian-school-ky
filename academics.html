Our Academic Programs

Calvary Christian School offers a strong academic curriculum across Elementary, Middle, and High School levels. Our programs focus on academic excellence, spiritual growth, and character development, preparing students for higher education and life.

Pre-K and Elementary School

Students build a solid foundation in reading, math, science, and Bible studies. Early years focus on developing curiosity, learning skills, and a love for God’s Word.

Middle School

Students develop critical thinking, responsibility, and leadership skills. Core subjects are combined with spiritual and character-building activities to grow well-rounded students.

High School

High School students are prepared for college and beyond with strong academics, Christian mentorship, and opportunities for leadership, service, and personal growth.
