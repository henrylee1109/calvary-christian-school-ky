Who We Are

Calvary Christian School is dedicated to providing Christ-centered education in Barbourville, Kentucky. Our school focuses on developing students academically, spiritually, and morally.

We foster a supportive environment where students grow in knowledge, character, and faith while preparing for success in life.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

To provide a Christ-centered education that nurtures academic excellence, spiritual growth, and strong moral character.

Vision

To cultivate students who live out their faith while excelling academically and serving their community.

Core Values

Faith

We prioritize spiritual growth through daily Bible learning and prayer.

Knowledge

We provide strong academics that prepare students for lifelong learning.

Character

We encourage integrity, responsibility, and kindness.
